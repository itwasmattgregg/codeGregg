  title: 'How To See All Participante In Google Meet'
  date: '2021-05-03T10:16:00Z'
---

Recently learned you can view up to 49 people silmultaneously in Google Meet.
Click the three dots in the bottom right corner and click "Change Layout". In
that modal click "Tiled" and move the slider all the way to the right. Now you
can look at everyone picking their nose at the same time!
